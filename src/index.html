I created a simple dice game for two players called Pig Game. The goal of the game is to reach a score of 20 before the opponent does. Each player rolls a die as many times as they want and adds the number obtained to their current score. But beware, if a player rolls a 1, their current score is lost and the turn passes to the opponent. Players can choose to hold their current score at any time by clicking on the 'Hold' button. The first player to reach a score of 20 or more wins the game.

I developed a small JavaScript game called 'Guess My Number' to offer players a fun and entertaining challenge. The game is very simple to play: you have to guess a random number between 1 and 20 using your limited guessing points. If you successfully guess the number, you'll get a high score.
